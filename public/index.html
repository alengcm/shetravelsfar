Our Purpose

Our Mission

We’re two women who love travel and believe every woman deserves the chance to experience the world safely, beautifully, and joyfully — surrounded by connection, laughter, and freedom.

Fully Inclusive.
Thoughtfully Designed.

Our journeys are land-only, fully inclusive experiences — created to remove the stress of planning and decision-making.

  • Boutique accommodation
  • Curated cultural activities
  • Meaningful shared experiences
  • Most or all meals
  • Seamless daily flow & Small group setting
  • Hosted and supported throughout

Flights are booked separately so you can travel from wherever you are. While flights are not included, Lauren — founder of The Point Whiz — can provide optional guidance on using existing points or selecting suitable paid fares to help you travel with confidence.

Meet The Founders

"We created She Travels Far because we wanted to travel better. Not faster, not cheaper—but deeper. With more connection and less worry."

Lauren | Co-Founder

The Logistics & Points Expert

Founder of The Point Whiz, Lauren brings technical precision to travel logistics. She believes luxury should be accessible and that the journey is just as important as the destination. Her passion lies in making complex itineraries feel effortless, ensuring you have the confidence to fly far and wide.

Celeste | Co-Founder

The Experience Curator

With a heart for culture and genuine human connection, Celeste curates the on-ground magic. She scouts the hidden gems, the boutique stays, and the local guides that turn a trip into a memory. Her focus is on safety, beauty, and ensuring every woman feels welcome and celebrated.
