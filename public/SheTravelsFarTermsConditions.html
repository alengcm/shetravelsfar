All prices are quoted in AUD unless stated otherwise. Prices may change due to factors outside our control such as currency fluctuations, supplier changes, taxes, or surcharges. 2. Deposits & Payments

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your place. This deposit covers planning time, administration, and early supplier expenses incurred by us.

Final payment is due no later than 90 days (3 months) prior to departure, unless otherwise advised.

For certain trips, full payment may be required earlier or later, depending on supplier terms and booking conditions. The applicable final payment date will be confirmed at the time of booking.

If full payment is not received by the specified due date, your booking may be cancelled and your deposit forfeited.

3. Cancellations & Refunds

We understand that unexpected situations can arise. Our policy aims to be fair while also protecting our small business and covering supplier costs.

If you cancel more than 3 months before departure

You will receive a full refund of all payments made,
minus the non-refundable deposit.
No additional cancellation fees apply.

If you cancel within 3 months of departure

At this stage, accommodation and activity suppliers typically require full, non-refundable payments from us.

However, we will always try to minimise your loss.

  • If we can fill your spot:
     You will receive a full refund of your trip balance,
    minus your deposit
     and any non-recoverable supplier fees (if applicable).
  • If we cannot fill your spot:
     The trip becomes non-refundable.

If She Travels Far must cancel

If minimum numbers are not reached or unexpected circumstances prevent the trip from going ahead, you will receive:

  • a full refund, or
  • the option to move to another confirmed date.

We are not responsible for flights or other external expenses not booked through She Travels Far.
