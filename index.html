Our Purpose

Our Mission

We’re two women who love travel and believe every woman deserves the chance to experience the world safely, beautifully, and joyfully — surrounded by connection, laughter, and freedom.

Fully Inclusive.
Thoughtfully Designed.

Our journeys are land-only, fully inclusive experiences — created to remove the stress of planning and decision-making.

  • Boutique accommodation
  • Curated cultural activities
  • Meaningful shared experiences
  • Most or all meals
  • Seamless daily flow & Small group setting
  • Hosted and supported throughout

Flights are booked separately so you can travel from wherever you are. While flights are not included, Lauren — founder of The Point Whiz — can provide optional guidance on using existing points or selecting suitable paid fares to help you travel with confidence.
