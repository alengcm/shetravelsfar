🌿 She Travels Far — Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 — Last updated: 3 December 2025

She Travels Far (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store and share your information when you visit our website, join our mailing list, make a booking, or participate in one of our hosted travel experiences.

We handle your information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. What Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you enquire, book, travel or interact with us:

Personal identification

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Postal address
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Travel-related information

  • Passport details
  • Dietary requirements
  • Medical information relevant to your safety
  • Travel insurance details
  • Emergency contact information
  • Frequent flyer program information (if you request points guidance)

Payment information

Processed securely through third-party payment providers such as Stripe.
We do not store full credit card numbers.

Website data

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Browsing behaviour
  • Cookies (if enabled)

Communications

  • Emails or messages you send us
  • Booking notes
  • Feedback after your trip

2. How We Collect Your Information

We may collect information when you:

  • submit a booking form
  • join our email list
  • pay a deposit or final balance
  • browse our website
  • communicate with us via email, social media or messaging apps
  • request reward-flight assistance from The Point Whiz
  • participate in any She Travels Far trip

3. Why We Collect Your Information (Purpose of Use)

We collect and use your information to:

Deliver your trip

  • secure accommodation, transport and activities
  • manage dietary requirements
  • organise room allocations
  • send trip updates and communication

Ensure safety & wellbeing

  • identify relevant medical needs
  • confirm your travel insurance
  • assist in emergencies

Administration & payments

  • process bookings and payments
  • issue invoices and receipts
  • manage cancellations and changes

Optional flight guidance via The Point Whiz

  • provide educational information about Classic Rewards
  • help you understand your points options
    (We do not book flights for you.)

Marketing & communications

  • send trip announcements and newsletters
  • provide early access to future tours
    You can unsubscribe at any time.

Website improvement

  • understand user behaviour
  • improve website performance and user experience

We do not use your information for any purpose you would not reasonably expect.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

We may disclose essential information to trusted third parties such as:

  • hotels and accommodation providers
  • tour guides and activity suppliers
  • transport operators
  • booking platforms
  • insurance providers (if needed)
  • payment processors (e.g., Stripe)
  • email marketing services (e.g., MailerLite or similar)

Only relevant information is shared — for example:

  • dietary needs for meal planning
  • passport names for activity reservations
  • emergency information only if required

We do not sell or trade your personal data.

5. How We Store & Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including:

  • secure password-protected systems
  • encrypted payment processing
  • restricted staff access
  • two-factor authentication where possible
  • secure cloud storage solutions

Information is retained only for as long as necessary for travel, safety, legal or operational business purposes.

6. Accessing or Correcting Your Information

You may request to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you
  • correct inaccuracies
  • request deletion where legally allowable

To submit a request, email:
📧 admin@shetravelsfar.com

We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Overseas Disclosure

Because She Travels Far operates international trips, we may disclose limited personal information to overseas suppliers such as:

  • hotels
  • transport companies
  • activity providers
  • local guides

These countries may not have privacy laws equivalent to Australia.
By booking with us, you consent to this disclosure.

8. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve performance.

Cookies may collect:

  • IP addresses
  • browser type
  • pages visited
  • session duration

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. Marketing Communications

If you opt in, we may send you:

  • newsletters
  • updates
  • trip announcements
  • promotions

You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in the email or by contacting us directly.

10. Complaints

If you believe your privacy has been breached, please contact us at admin@shetravelsfar.com.
We will investigate and respond promptly.

If you are dissatisfied with our response, you may contact:

Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)
 www.oaic.gov.au

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always appear on our website.

12. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, or to request access or correction, please contact:

📧 admin@shetravelsfar.com
 🌐 www.shetravelsfar.com
